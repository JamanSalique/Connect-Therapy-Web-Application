What we do

People may go through some life-changing events that could have long-term effects on their mental health and make them more vulnerable to common disorders. Therapy can be used to help cope with these problems and can be used for all ages, it will have positive changes on your health and well being as you can work out where your negative feelings are coming from and why they are there.

The types of conditions that we treat

Anxiety disorder, Depression, Phobias, Post-traumatic stress disorder, Panic disorder, Anger, Bullying, Fear, Stress, Emotional, behavioural and psychological problems, and other similar issues
